Our History

Kentucky Fried Chicken (KFC) was founded by Colonel Harland Sanders in 1952. From a humble roadside restaurant in Corbin, Kentucky, KFC has grown into one of the largest fast-food chains in the world, serving millions of customers daily.

Our Mission

At KFC, our mission is to provide delicious, high-quality food that brings people together. We are committed to serving our customers with the best fried chicken and sides, ensuring a memorable dining experience.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the freshest ingredients in our recipes.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and respect.
  • Community: We believe in giving back to the communities we serve.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek to improve and innovate our menu.
